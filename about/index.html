About LyricBubble

⚠ Proof of Concept — Archived Prototype

This site was an early experimental prototype created to explore a user-voted music chart concept based on song lyrics. Development has been discontinued and the site is left online for demonstration purposes only. Features may be incomplete or non-functional, and the data may not be maintained.

The concept

LyricBubble was a community-driven lyric voting experiment. The idea: surface the lines from songs that people find most memorable, clever, or moving — right now, not five years ago.

How voting worked

Each day you get 5 votes to spread across the chart — one vote per lyric per day. Votes are anonymous; no accounts or personal data are collected.

Why votes fade

A lyric that dominated last month shouldn't crowd out what's resonating today. Votes decay over a 7-day window, so the chart stays fresh and reflects current listening rather than historical momentum.
