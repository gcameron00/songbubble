About SongBubble

The concept

SongBubble is a community-driven song voting site. The idea: surface the songs people are actually listening to and loving right now — not five years ago.

Anyone can nominate a song and vote for their current favourites. The main chart shows the current Top 10, with album artwork for higher-placed entries. Historic charts let you look back at what was bubbling at any point in the past.

How voting works

Each day you get 5 votes to spread across the chart. Votes are anonymous — no account or personal data is required.

Why votes fade

A song that dominated last month shouldn’t crowd out what’s resonating today. Votes decay over a 28-day window, so the chart stays fresh and reflects current listening rather than historical momentum.

Coming soon

We’re planning catalogue search via Apple Music and Spotify, so you can find and nominate songs without typing everything by hand. For users with accounts on those services, in-page playback will follow.
